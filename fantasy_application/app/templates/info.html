How it Works

The stat projections are made by retrieving the average stats of the players on the specified teams, using the specified stat type. The projections also take into account injury status and schedule (ie. whether the player is playing or not). A player is considered injured on a given date if his current injury status is different from ACTIVE. The exception is that if a player's status is DAY-TO-DAY, the player is considered to be ACTIVE 2 days from the current date.
